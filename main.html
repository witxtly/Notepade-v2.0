Notepad is a very simple program on your computer for writing text. Think of it as a digital piece of paper. You can open it, type a shopping list, a phone number, or a quick reminder, and save it. It does only the basics: you can type, delete, and copy text, but you can't make it colorful, change the font style, or add pictures. It's just for plain, simple words. Because it's so simple, it opens very fast and doesn't confuse you with too many buttons.
